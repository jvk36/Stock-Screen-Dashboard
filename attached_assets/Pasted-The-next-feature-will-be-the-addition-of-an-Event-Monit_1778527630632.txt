The next feature will be the addition of an Event Monitor tab. It is different from the other tabs in that each event will need a separate filter to get a list of stocks. Most of the events would be SEC filings-based or other public information like PR and such. Here is a list of events to monitor: a) Index Additions: tracks stocks that are to be included in the major indices within the next few months, including the S&P 500, S&P MidCap 400, S&P SmallCap 600, Nasdaq 100, etc. - Index additions create forced buying from passive funds, which can drive price appreciation around the effective date – show PPS before announcement, current price, timing details, etc., b) Index Exits: tracks stocks that are set to exit the major indices – show PPS and timing details, c) M&A Arbitrage: Tracks acquisitions after announcement by monitoring deal spreads, structure (cash vs. stock), timeline, and regulatory milestones, d) M&A Cancellations: tracks deals from the trailing twelve months that have been canceled, withdrawn, or terminated – monitor pre-deal price, price of the deal, premium/discount to current PPS, etc. - the PPS can dislocate and trade at a sharp discount to pre-deal price, e) Active Activist Campaigns – Monitor 13D filings for filings by the following activist investors - Elliott Investment Management (Paul Singer), Carl Icahn, Third Point (Dan Loeb), Starboard Value, Value Act, Pershing Square, Jana Partners, Engaged Capital, Greenlight Capital, Eminence Capital, Cannell Capital, etc. – show PPS before the filing, the current price, and the premium/discount, f) Spin-offs: Announced but not yet closed – there can be opportunities in both the parent as well as the spin depending on the situation – show PPS before announcement, Deal Price, Details (cash/stock/combo), etc., g) Bankruptcy Exits – Monitor companies in Chapter 11 – there may be opportunity, as Chapter 11 offers companies the breathing room they need to reorganize their debts while continuing operations, h) Up-listings: monitors stocks scheduled to move from OTC to a major exchange – show price and timing, i) Short Squeezes – monitor stocks that have short-interest going up in the previous week and has exceeded a threshold of 10%, j) Down-listings: monitors stocks scheduled to move from major exchanges to OTC – show price and timing, k) IPO Unlocks: Tracks stocks that have IPO unlocks happening within the next 3 months – show the IPO Price, Current Price, IPO Raise, Current Float, Float after IPO Unlock, etc., l) CEO Turnover – tracks appointment of a new CEO – show compensation details, m) Cluster Insider Buys – tracks open market purchases of stock by multiple insiders of a public company, including top executives like the CEO, CFO, COO, and Directors, n) Investor Days – tracks investor days coming up within the next month – show the previous investor day and any specific theme for the investor day.
